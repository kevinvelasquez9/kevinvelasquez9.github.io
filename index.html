Heat Distribution

This Java program will simulate and visualize the heat distribution across a metal plate. The plate has restricted cells that are either the coldest or hottest points on the plate. The unrestricted cells are free to change, by taking the average of its surrounding cells. The simulation will continue until two iterations have data within epsilon of each other.
